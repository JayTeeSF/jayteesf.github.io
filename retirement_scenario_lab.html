    Build + usage notes

    This prototype is not tax, legal, investment, retirement, or financial advice. It is a planning sandbox for educational and entertainment purposes only. Verify every scenario, assumption, and tax result with a qualified CPA, EA, CFP, or attorney before taking action. Real-world outcomes depend on facts this prototype does not model fully, including future law changes, exact benefit elections, lot-level tax treatment, state residency rules, timing nuances, and software defects.
