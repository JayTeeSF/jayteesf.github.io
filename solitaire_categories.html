How to play

Collect every visible topic card into the correct category targets across the top.

  • Tap the stock to draw one card into the waste.
  • Tap a visible card to select it, then tap a valid target.
  • You can move a same-category run onto an empty tableau column or onto another top card of the same category.
  • Double-tap a card to auto-place it onto its category when that move is legal.
  • When a full category is complete, it glows and locks in.
Unexpected mixes Every game pulls from a large category library, so you may see planets next to breakfast, retro tech, mythology, gems, or circus props.
Visual + word cards Some cards are mostly text, some lean visual, and many use both so the board stays lively and surprising.

Progress is autosaved in this browser so you can leave and come back later.
