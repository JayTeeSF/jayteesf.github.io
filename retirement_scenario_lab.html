    Accounts

    Enter a current snapshot. Transactions are optional unless you want deeper spending calibration.

    Account name Owner Type Balance Cost basis Rate / return Dividend yield Term yrs Sched. pmt Extra princ. Notes
    Import format is simple JSON. A server-side Monarch adapter can emit the same schema by mapping accounts into checking, taxable, ira, roth, and mortgage buckets and carrying cost basis for taxable assets. Mortgage balances are entered as the principal still owed, not as a negative number. Mortgage rows can now also carry a rate, remaining term, scheduled annual payment, and extra annual principal so the engine can model amortization instead of simple debt accretion.
