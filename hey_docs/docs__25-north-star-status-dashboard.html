Dependency baseline

The semantic audit covers 157 maintained files and 1,241 executable-position dependencies. Implementation scope is intentionally small and exact: one migration-required `curl`, one Darwin `codesign`, and one Darwin `xattr`. Non-Darwin targets bypass the trust commands; they are accepted platform boundaries rather than global prerequisites.

Command scope remains the larger migration inventory: 44 `awk`, 76 `cat`, 35 `grep`, 35 `mkdir`, 19 `rm`, 23 `tr`, and 48 thin `sh` launchers. Verification scope remains separate: 693 `grep`, 8 `cp`, 48 `rm`, 33 `mkdir`, and 22 `make` locations. Counts are inventory, not completion scores.
