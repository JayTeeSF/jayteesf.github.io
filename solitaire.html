How to play

Goal: move all cards to the four foundation piles, building each suit from Ace up to King.

  1. Tap the stock pile to draw cards into the waste pile.
  2. Build down in the tableau by alternating colors.
  3. Move Kings to empty tableau columns.
  4. Move Aces to the foundations, then continue upward in the same suit.
  5. Tap or double-tap a movable card to auto-place it when there is an obvious legal move.

The game supports tap play, drag-and-drop, autosave, export/import, and undo.

Install on your phone

On iPhone or iPad, Safari still uses the Add to Home Screen flow for installing a web app.

  1. Open this game in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Rename it if you want.
  5. Confirm the add action.

Once added, it launches more like an app and will not keep opening normal browser tabs during play.
