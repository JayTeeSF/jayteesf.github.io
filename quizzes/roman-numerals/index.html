Roman Numerals

Convert integers from 1 through 3999 to canonical Roman numerals and convert valid canonical Roman numerals back to integers.

conversionvalidationgreedy algorithms

Your task

  • Emit subtractive forms such as IV, IX, XL, XC, CD, and CM.
  • Reject zero, negative, and values above 3999.
  • Reject non-canonical or malformed Roman input.
  • Round-trip every accepted value.

Example shape

29 -> XXIX
CCXCI -> 291
1999 -> MCMXCIX

Approach ideas

These are strategy summaries, not copied Ruby solutions.

  • Greedily consume descending numeral/value pairs for encoding.
  • Parse pairs before single symbols for decoding.
  • Validate decoding by re-encoding and comparing with the input.
