GEDCOM Parser

Parse GEDCOM lines whose leading level numbers describe hierarchy. Build a tree that preserves tags, optional identifiers, values, and parent-child relationships, then serialize the result as structured data.

parsingtreesformats

Your task

  • Reject malformed level numbers.
  • A line may increase by only one level at a time.
  • Preserve repeated tags and ordering.
  • Do not confuse pointer identifiers with ordinary values.

Example shape

0 @I1@ INDI
1 NAME Ada /Lovelace/
1 BIRT
2 DATE 10 DEC 1815

Approach ideas

These are strategy summaries, not copied Ruby solutions.

  • Maintain a stack of the most recent node at each level.
  • First produce flat records, then perform a separate tree-building pass.
  • Stream large files and emit completed subtrees when their level closes.
