How to play

Stack falling pieces to complete horizontal lines. Cleared lines raise your score and level, which speeds up the game.

  1. Move pieces left and right to fit gaps.
  2. Rotate pieces before they lock.
  3. Use Hold to save one piece for later, once per drop.
  4. Hard drop when you’re sure — it lands instantly and scores a bit more.
  5. Use the settings wheel for backup, restore, feedback, install help, and the walkthrough.

The game supports keyboard controls, touch buttons, board gestures, autosave, export/restore, and haptics.

Install on your phone

On iPhone or iPad, Safari still uses the Add to Home Screen flow for installing a web app.

  1. Open this game in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Rename it if you want.
  5. Confirm the add action.

Once added, it launches more like an app and keeps the experience cleaner during repeat play.
