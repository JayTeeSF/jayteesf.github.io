Printable Memory Game Sets

Download and print these 3×3 card sets for a simple memory-order game. Each set has a front PDF (card faces) and a matching back PDF (card backs with the set name). Print the fronts, then re-feed the same sheets to print the backs on the other side. Cut along the lines to create your deck.

How to Play

Most sets include a rules card, but the core idea is simple: shuffle the nine cards and place them face-down in a 3×3 grid. Before starting, decide whether everyone will use the same ordering or whether each player may choose a valid order (Bible verse, alphabetical, numeric, or a personal order).

On your turn, you may either explore by flipping a single new card, or attempt to flip the full sequence in order. If you ever flip a card out of order, your turn ends and all cards are turned face-down again. The first player to reveal the entire sequence in the correct order in a single turn wins.
